Contributing

If you wish to contribute to Sanctum, please send your patches to <priests snabel-a sanctorum punkt se>

Your mail should include a proper and understandable reasoning behind the patch and why it is important. Patches without reason are immediately rejected. Keep your mails short and to the point.

Please follow the established style when adding new code.
