Topologies

Sanctum out of the box, supports multiple traditional and more novel modern topologies.

Traditional topologies

  • Site-to-site tunnels, classic VPN.
  • Client-to-site tunnels, classic roadwarrior.

Novel topologies

  • Peer autodiscovery and full mesh mode.
  • Automatic hub-spoke, even with multiple hubs.
  • Unidirectional tunnels, in case of diodes in the path.
  • Device-to-Device tunnels, P2P and E2EE even behind NAT and firewalls.

Roaming

Roaming just works in sanctum, tunnels update their peer endpoints automatically.
